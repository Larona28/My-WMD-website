Professional Eco-Cleaning Services

Strict ecological standard matrices built for various structural capacities in Botswana.

1. Residential Complexes & Estates

Our residential matrix handles private homes, estates, and high-density apartments with maximum sanitization focus. Includes micro-allergic pollen remediation, deep bio-carpet extraction wash, upholstery cleaning, and thorough eco-scrubbing parameters for kitchens and bathrooms.

2. Commercial Corporate Workspaces

High-efficiency maintenance layouts for corporate offices, retail spaces, and bank branches in Gaborone. Engineered to mitigate workplace illness trends by targeting high-contact architectural touchpoints like server rooms, desktops, elevator banks, and common kitchen layouts without chemical scents.

3. Institutional & Medical Sanitation

Rigorous clinical sanitization workflows designed for medical facilities, academic lecture halls, and daycare centers. We deploy premium botanical surface sanitizers with zero toxic vapors, eliminating bacteria and pathogens while ensuring an optimal, allergen-free environment for vulnerable building occupants.

4. Outdoor & Solar Architecture

Specialized outdoor maintenance engineered for semi-arid environments. This workflow clears dust accumulation from photovoltaic (PV) solar panels to restore solar capture efficiency. It also provides zero-runoff brick cleaning and eco-friendly property wash treatments to manage harsh desert soot.
