The EcoShine Operations Story

Pioneering sustainable environmental sanitation frameworks in Botswana since 2022.

Our Journey to Sustainable Cleanliness

EcoShine Cleaning was established to address the growing need for high-efficacy sanitation that doesn't compromise local water tables or indoor air quality. Operating from Gaborone, our mission is to eliminate hazardous chemical cleaners from residential and commercial routines.

By leveraging localized plant extracts and advanced microfiber capture technology, we deliver thorough disinfection workflows. Our practices protect property structural surfaces while ensuring a safe environment for your team and family.
